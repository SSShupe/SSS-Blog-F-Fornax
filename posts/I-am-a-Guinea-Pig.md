I will be participating in a Phase 1 trial at Stanford for a new "in vivo" CAR-T treatment against multiple myeloma.

The new product has been tested on a handful of people in Australia, and all are doing well. They had nominal side effects from the infusion process and their myeloma is under control.

I know that risks exist from partcipating in the trial, but after discussing it with my doctors at Kaiser and Stanford and conducting a good amount of research, I concluded the risks were acceptable given the likely benefits.

I go into the hospital at Stanford on May 3 and get the infusion of the treatment on May 4. I'll be in the hospital for 9 to 12 days and Suzie and I have to spend the next 2.5 to 3 weeks close to the Stanford hospital.

I will use this blog to keep everyone updated. Good thoughts, wishes, etc. are appreciated.
