I Am in Control of the East Bay

Steve Shupe on 2026-01-03

Our destructive, demented President and his crew of happy killers/destroyers rolled into Venezuela last night and kidnapped its President and his wife. Now maybe Trump's ignorant, uneducated MAGA followers will believe that the US now controls Venezuela, but anyone with half a brain who looks at a map of the country will realize the absurdity of that claim. Venezuela is enormous. (The old Mercator projection maps diminish the apparent size of land closer to the equator, remember?) Over 350,000 square miles of land (like Texas and Oklahoma put together), 29 million people, 1,700 miles of coastline, mountains, and jungles.

Exactly how many US troops are there now?

But what the hell, "control" is all in the mind, isn't it? Trump thinks he controls Venezuela now. Well guess what?

I control the entire East Bay. From Crockett all the way out to Tracy, it's mine, baby. And I'm gonna extract some resources, yes sir. I'm the hottest resident in California now.
